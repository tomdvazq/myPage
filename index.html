First of all, thank you for visiting my site.

My name is Tomás, I'm 23 years old and I'm from Argentina.

Since I finished high school I went through many places to get here.

The IT world has always been close to me, but for a long time I resisted getting to know it in depth.

But always, sooner or later, one arrives at the port where one must arrive.

How did I learn about programming?

My first approach was at the age of nine when I set up a personal version of Habbo Hotel,

a multiplayer game that had released its code so that players could create their own versions.

Studies

I started studying in a self-taught way via YouTube with various tutorials that I found on the platform,

thanks to this I got to know freeCodeCamp and took the HTML and CSS course.

It was in March 2022 that I decided to get serious and started studying at CoderHouse academy,

getting certified as a web developer.

Currently I continue to take courses at CoderHouse and have created a self-taught habit of learning.
